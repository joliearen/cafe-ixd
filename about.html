About Us

Cafe Conception d'Expérience was established by an IXD graduate student, around the same time that she started her first semester at the Pratt Manhattan campus. While working on a client project with a local Manhattan roastery, she realized the need for an in-house cafe at the very building that her and her fellow classmates study in. In her spare time between classes, you can spot the student brewing the latest and freshest pour overs in the city.

The cafe is located near West Village and also houses the School of Information. Though the cafe focuses its efforts on single origin coffee, below are some of the food offerings by the cafe, courtesy of Pratt event caterers. Vegetarian friendly!
